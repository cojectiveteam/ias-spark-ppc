About IAS Spark

IAS Spark is a mentorship-based learning initiative designed to help school students develop leadership thinking and awareness of the world around them. Through structured discussions, real-world topics, and guided mentorship, students learn to

Think critically

Develop analytical minds that question and understand
Encourage critical thinking to question, analyze, and reason clearly

Communicate confidently

Express ideas clearly with confidence and clarity
Build strong communication skills to speak and present effectively

Understand society and governance

Develop awareness of society, systems, and how governance works
Understand real-world structures and civic responsibilities

Build leadership qualities

Develop leadership skills to guide, influence, and take initiative
Build confidence to lead with responsibility and decision-making
